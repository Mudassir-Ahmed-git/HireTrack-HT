# 📌 HireTrack — Job Application Tracker

HireTrack is a personal CRM designed to simplify and organize your job search. It helps you track every application, follow your progress, and never miss a follow-up again.

Think of it as your **command center for job hunting** — from first application to final offer.


## 🚀 Features

* 🗂️ **Kanban Board Workflow**

  * Visualize your job pipeline with drag-and-drop cards
  * Stages: *Applied → Interview → Offer → Rejected*

* 🏢 **Application Tracking**

  * Store company name, role, and application details
  * Add custom notes for each opportunity

* ⏰ **Follow-up Reminders**

  * Track important dates and upcoming follow-ups
  * Stay consistent with recruiter communication

* 📊 **Dashboard Analytics**

  * Overview of your job search progress
  * Visual insights into applications by status

* 📤 **CSV Export**

  * Export your data anytime for backup or external use

---

## 🧠 Why HireTrack?

Job hunting is messy. Applications get lost, follow-ups are forgotten, and opportunities slip through the cracks.

HireTrack solves this by bringing structure, visibility, and discipline to your entire job search process.

It’s inspired by real-world CRM systems used in enterprise software — making it both practical and highly transferable as a developer project.
